Publications

As a member of the LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA Collaboration, as well as a key contributor to the LIGO Detector Characterization working group, I have the privilege of appearing on numerous publications where I did not directly contribute to the study design, data analysis or paper writing by virtue of my contributions to producing low-noise gravitational wave data in the third and fourth observing runs of LIGO. Therefore, I only list the subset of my publications here where I directly contributed to the text. A full list of publications may be found on my ORCID page. Last updated: December 9, 2025.
