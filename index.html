    I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Oregon. I study gravitational wave astrophysics and detector systematics. In particular, I'm interested in gravitational wave bursts from novel sources, especially cosmic strings. I'm also interested in improving our ability to discriminate between real gravitational waves and environmental noise in the LIGO detectors.
